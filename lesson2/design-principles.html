Design Principles Document

Rule
of
Threes

Jimmy John's

The Jimmy John's home page demonstrates the princple of the Rule of Threes. The page is segmented such that the navigation bar is separated from the pickup and delivery section which is separate from the image of the sandwich with the order now button.

Visual

Hierarchy

BioLife Plasma

The BioLife Plasma page above demonstrates Visual Hierarchy by the way that the headings are formated. Your eyes are immediately drawn to the "Are you eligible to donate?" header.

White Space
and
Clean Design

LinkedIn

LinkedIn demonstrates the principle of White Space and Clean Design with it's home page. The graphics and text are spaced such that there is adequate room between the elements and there is no over crowding on the page.
