Site Name

The site name will be My Current Local Weather with the domain of mycurrentlocalweather.com. The cost of this domain is $12/year from Google Domains.

Site Purpose

The purpose of mycurrentlocalweather.com is to provide users with the weather news for the cities of Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven while providing other relevant weather information.
