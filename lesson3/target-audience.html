Audience Description

The target audience of the mycurrentlocalweather.com website would be the everyday on-the-go person that needs to quickly know the weather.

  • Who:Travelers, locals, parents, after school coordinators and anyone that plans events that could be effectedd by the weather.
  • Age:10-100
  • Income:All/any
  • Motivation:Be able to access the weather forcast for their city and see how community events are effected by the weather if applicable.

Scenarios

How is the weather effecting the roads?

  • The weather is displayed along with a link to the department of transportation website where there is a map of the road conditions.

Is the Farmer's Market still happening if it's raining?

  • The weather is displayed along with a link to the website/ facebook page with the information about the farmers market

There's a blizzard coming, what do I need to do to be prepared?

  • Display the updates on the storm and link out to the American Red Cross page for disaster safety and preparations for cold weather storms.

What's the forecast for the day?

  • Display a very simple hour by hour forecast of the day.

What time is sunset?

  • Display the current time and the estimated time of sunset with a count down till that time.
